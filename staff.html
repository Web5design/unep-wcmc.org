The team

We employ a technical team of around 80 scientists and technicians, most of whom are educated to Masters or Doctorate level. A small but high-quality administrative team work alongside them to keep the Centre running smoothly. We are constantly on the look-out for talented people to join our team, so if you think you would fit, send us your CV.
