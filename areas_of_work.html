Our expertise

At UNEP-WCMC we develop and share knowledge and tools to enable people to understand how they depend and impact upon biodiversity. We work actively with people to ensure that they can – and do – use these tools effectively. We help people to explicitly account for the value of biodiversity in policy- and decision-making for development and the environment.

In dozens of projects every year we work with our partners to strengthen the foundations of good environmental management in the following ways:

  • we help create a comprehensive knowledge base
  • we support objective assessments and valuations of biodiversity and ecosystem services
  • we build tools and methodologies for understanding the environment
  • we support people to develop policies and institutions to drive change
  • we share the skills and competencies to do these better.

Our growing team comprises more than a hundred experts in the biodiversity and ecosystem services of marine and terrestrial environments, as well as social scientists, ecological modellers, GIS specialists, policy analysts and programmers. Together we specialize in measuring and identifying the causes of biodiversity change, mapping biodiversity hotspots and priorities, valuing ecosystem services, and evaluating the implications for biodiversity of different land and marine management options.
