Areas of work

We collate, verify, analyse and interpret data on biodiversity and ecosystem services, often involving large and complex datasets and analyses.

We provide this information in a range of accessible and appropriate forms for national and international level decision-makers, businesses and practitioners involved in biodiversity management.

Our work spans terrestrial and marine environments, and often overlaps several of these themes.
