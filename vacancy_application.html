Projects by Protected Areas

EU Wildlife Trade Regulations

UNEP-WCMC provides a wide range of services to the European Commission in support of the effective implementation of the EU Wildlife Trade Regulations, the legislation that provides for the enforcement of CITES within the European Union.

Biodiversity Indicators Partnership

The CBD-mandated Biodiversity Indicators Partnership (BIP) is a global initiative that has operated since 2007, promoting and coordinating development and delivery of biodiversity indicators in support of the CBD and related Conventions, national and regional governments and a range of other sectors.
